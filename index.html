Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh excels in innovations like digital payments, eco-friendly jute products, and sustainable energy, showcasing resilience and creativity globally.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures balanced growth by conserving resourc s, promoting renewable energy, reducing waste, supporting equality, and protecting future generations.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure forms the backbone of development, encompassing transportation, energy, water, communication, and healthcare systems, enabling societal growth and connectivity

Empowerment

Empowerment fosters confidence, skills, and opportunities, enabling individuals or communities to take control of their lives and drive positive change.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.
